Who We Are

Built from a university
dream into reality.

Bulbycode was founded by a student from the University of Johannesburg's Academy of Computer Sciences and Software Engineering. What began as a vision to bridge the gap between township entrepreneurs and the digital world has grown into a full-service digital transformation agency.

We understand the unique challenges faced by small and micro-businesses in South Africa, limited budgets, limited tech literacy, and limited time. That's why we offer end-to-end digital services that are affordable, and built for real people.

Our Core Mission: Digital Inclusion

We believe every business, regardless of size or location, deserves access to the digital economy. Bulbycode exists to make that a reality for South African small businesses.
